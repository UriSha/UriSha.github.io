Uri Shaham

Tel Aviv University

I am a Rsearch Scientist at Google Research and a Computer Science PhD candidate at Tel Aviv University, researching Natural Language Processing with my advisor Omer Levy. My recent research focuses on long-range text understanding and multilingual instruction tuning and translation.
